Discover the Elegant Rooms of Mermaid Cove Resort

Mermaid Cove Resort is the perfect holiday village where dreams come true and you can immerse yourself in the captivating depths of the sea. Now, embark on a memorable experience with three different room options offered to you

  • Sea View Room

    This luxurious and spacious suite room offers an unforgettable accommodation experience. Equipped with a comfortable bed, seating area, and private balcony or terrace, it is a perfect choice for guests who want to hear the soothing sounds of the sea and enjoy the magnificent sea view.

  • Pool View Room

    This room provides a comfortable stay with a refreshing pool view. It is equipped with a comfortable bed, seating area, and modern amenities. An ideal option for guests who want to enjoy swimming in the pool and relax.

  • Garden View Room

    These rooms offer a comfortable accommodation with a view of the hotel's beautiful gardens. They are equipped with a comfortable bed, seating area, and amenities to meet your needs. Suitable for guests who want to relax in a peaceful atmosphere and enjoy nature.
